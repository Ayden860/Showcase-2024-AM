DinoBites Express

Welcome to DinoBites Express, where prehistoric cravings meet modern tastes. At DinoBites Express, we're on a mission to transport you back to a time when the dinosaurs roamed the Earth, all while satisfying your contemporary cravings for fast, delicious food.

Our Menu

Our menu is a time-traveling adventure for your taste buds. We offer a wide range of prehistorically inspired dishes, from Dino Nugget Platters to Triceratot Tater Tots and Pterodactyl Pizzas. Whether you're a carnivore or an herbivore, we have something on our menu to tickle your taste buds.


But we don't stop at the food! Our drink menu boasts "Dino Delight" shakes and "Jurassic Juice" to quench your thirst, and our sweet treats, like "Fossil Rock Candy" and "Dino DNA Desserts," provide the perfect conclusion to your dino-themed meal.

Fast Food Restaurants

On our private island Isla Nublar, we have a wide selection of fast food restaurants to meet our visitor’s needs. McDonald's goal is to make delicious feel-good moments easy for everyone. Sonic Drive-In’s goal is to become America's most- loved restaurant brand. KFC’s goal is to sell food in a fast, friendly environment that appeals to pride conscious, health minded consumers. Pizza Hut’s goal is to make the perfect pizza and provide courteous and helpful service on time all the time.
